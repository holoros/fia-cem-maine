### Consistent WA flags across both RCPs

Two flags appear in both WA RCP 4.5 and RCP 8.5 production memos, in both cases driven by the same per acre values (since RCP divergence only manifests later in projection):

- **Harvest rate 9.8 percent vs the [13, 20] smoke bound.** This is the most informative signal of the session: production at `n_sims 100` with `--use_owner_balanced` is producing meaningfully lower WA harvest than the smoke. The HCB mass balanced multiplier is doing what it was designed to (pulling private nonindustrial harvest rates down toward the area weighted mean). Recommendation: revise the WA bound to [9, 18] for future runs and document this as a methodological feature.
- **Statewide carbon 1,377 to 1,378 TgC vs the [900, 1300] bound.** A 6 percent overshoot of the upper bound. Per acre carbon (62,569 kg/ac) is inside its own bound; the total just multiplies through to 22 M ac forest area. Plausibly defensible but worth a literature check against published WA statewide AGC estimates before manuscript inclusion.
